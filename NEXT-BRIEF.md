## Islamic geometric pattern construction (research notes)
- All patterns built from circles: start with circle grid, draw construction lines
  between intersection points, extract final pattern by selecting segments.
- 8-fold (octagonal) chosen for: Rub el Hizb (✳), 8 angels bearing the Throne,
  8 vertices of the cube (our hero object), ubiquity in Islamic architectural art.
- Tawhid (unity): all pattern elements radiate from a single center point.
- Infinity: pattern tiles seamlessly with no beginning or end.
- The octagram {8/2}: two squares overlapping at 45° — the fundamental shape.

- **Line weight**: 2.0px + blur. If too faint, try 2.5 or increase stroke alpha
