## Techniques & Research
- **FBO Dichroic Refraction** — Per-channel IOR technique inspired by [Patricio Gonzalez Vivo](https://patriciogonzalezvivo.com/) and [The Book of Shaders](https://thebookofshaders.com/)
- **Polar Disc Prayer Windows** — Custom angular shader for prayer time visualization
- **HRTF Spatial Audio** — Web Audio API PannerNode with compass-driven azimuth
- **Prismatic Fan Shader** — Piecewise spectral interpolation with Gaussian radial falloff
