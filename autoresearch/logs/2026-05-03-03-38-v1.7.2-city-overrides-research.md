## Research outcomes per cluster

### ✅ VERIFIED — Lucknow (India)

- **Institutional source:** All India Muslim Personal Law Board (AIMPLB) + Jamiat Ulema-e-Hind. Lucknow Hanafi-Deobandi institutional centre (Nadwatul Ulama, Firangi Mahal, Darul Uloom Nadwatul Ulama).
- **Method:** Karachi 18°/18° + Hanafi Asr.
- **Cross-references:** IslamicFinder Lucknow default, Muslim Pro, urdupoint.com all show "University of Islamic Sciences Karachi (Hanafi)" as the standard.
- **altMethods:** Tehran (for Lucknow's historic Twelver Shia minority — Awadh / Nawabi heritage; Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara — ~30% of Lucknow's Muslim population).
- **Override shipped:** `Lucknow → Karachi` (no change to calculation; explicit traceability via AIMPLB, surfaces Shia minority).

### ✅ VERIFIED — Kochi (Kerala, India)

- **Institutional source:** Samastha Kerala Jem-iyyathul Ulama (https://samastha.info/, est. 1925). Site explicitly states: "In contrast to the rest of Muslims in India, Kerala Muslims observe the Shafi'i school of law."
- **Method:** Karachi 18°/18° + **Shafi Asr** (shadow = 1× object).
- **Cross-references:** IslamicFinder Kerala state default, hamariweb.com Kerala default, multiple third-party apps confirm Karachi method + Standard (Shafi) Asr for Kerala Mappila Sunni Shafi'i communities (~88L Muslims, ~26% of Kerala state).
- **altMethods:** Karachi (Hanafi Asr) for Hanafi-minority North Indian diaspora communities living in Kerala.
- **Override shipped:** `Kochi → KarachiShafi` (new method-name in `methodFromString`, mirrors v1.7.1 Maldives + Sri Lanka explicit-Shafi pattern).

### ✅ VERIFIED — Cotabato (Maguindanao del Norte, BARMM)

- **Institutional source:** Bangsamoro Darul-Ifta' (BDI-BARMM, https://bdi.bangsamoro.gov.ph/) — official Islamic advisory council of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao. Issues Ramadan moon-sighting fatwas for the region.
- **Method:** MWL 18°/17° + Shafi Asr (matches Aladhan world-default for Philippines and adhan.js Shafi-default).
- **Cross-references:** al-hamdoulillah.com Cotabato default = MWL 18°/17° + Standard. Bangsamoro Filipino Muslims (Maguindanaon, Maranao, Tausug) follow Sunni Shafi'i per Islam in the Philippines literature.
- **altMethods:** ISNA (some Bangsamoro diaspora apps via NCMF Manila offices use ISNA 15°/15°).
- **Override shipped:** `Cotabato → MWL` (no change to calculation; explicit traceability via BDI-BARMM, same pattern as Bradford/Sarajevo).

### ✅ VERIFIED — Marawi (Lanao del Sur, BARMM)

- Same dispatch as Cotabato. Marawi is the principal Maranao city; Maranao tradition is Sunni Shafi'i.
- **Override shipped:** `Marawi → MWL` (BDI-BARMM institutional traceability).

### ❌ NOT VERIFIED — KRG (Sulaymaniyah, Erbil, Iraqi Kurdistan)

- **Institutional source attempted:** Kurdistan Regional Government Ministry of Endowment and Religious Affairs (https://gov.krd/moera-ar/, https://gov.krd/moera-en/). Ministry exists, but website does not publish prayer-time methodology (verified 2026-05-03; site returned 403/no-content for methodology pages).
- **Mawaqit search:** Found 1 mosque in Kalar (Sulaymaniyah area) and 1 in Harir (Erbil area) — neither exposed methodology in their public Mawaqit pages.
- **Community library:** `kurdistan-prayer-times-js` GitHub repo provides regional pre-computed timetables but does not document methodology or institutional sourcing (archived 2023).
- **Fiqh context:** Kurdish Sunni community is overwhelmingly Shafi'i (distinct from Iraqi Arab Sunni Hanafi). Third-party apps default Erbil/Sulaymaniyah to Karachi 18°/18° but do not cite KRG institutional sources.
- **Verdict:** Cannot ship without primary source per `feedback_aggressive_data_hunt`. Tracking: deeper data-hunt for v1.7.3+.

### ❌ NOT VERIFIED — Pattani (Thailand)

- **Institutional source attempted:** Sheikhul Islam Office of Thailand (Chularajamontri); Central Islamic Council of Thailand (CICOT, https://www.cicot.or.th/); Provincial Islamic Council of Pattani.
- CICOT website does not publish prayer-time methodology (verified 2026-05-03; "About" page covers organization structure, halal certification, but no calculation methodology).
- **Mawaqit search:** No Pattani-area mosques located on Mawaqit with methodology disclosure.
- **Fiqh context:** Pattani Muslims are Sunni Shafi'i Malay, culturally aligned with Kelantan/Malaysia (JAKIM 20°/18°). Third-party app defaults vary widely:
  - al-hamdoulillah.com Pattani default = MWL 18°/17° + Standard
  - Some apps use Mecca/Umm al-Qura preset (unusual)
  - No JAKIM 20°/18° default observed despite cultural alignment
- **Verdict:** Cannot ship without primary source. The conflicting third-party defaults make this especially risky to override without institutional backing. Tracking: Provincial Islamic Council Pattani direct contact / Wayback Machine for v1.7.3+.

### ❌ NOT SHIPPED — Chennai (Tamil Nadu, India)

Unlike Kochi (uniformly Shafi'i via Samastha Kerala), Tamil Nadu is multi-madhab:
- Coastal communities (Labbay, Marakkayar) are Shafi'i.
- Inland communities (Rowther) are Hanafi (Tamil Muslim ethnoreligious literature).
- No single institutional Imsakiyya source pins one method.

A Chennai override would force a single method on a population that legitimately splits between Hanafi and Shafi'i Asr — wrong choice. v1.7.3+ could add Chennai with altMethods exposing both, but a hard methodOverride is inappropriate without disambiguating the city's coastal-vs-inland sub-populations.
