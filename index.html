Introducing

A Gift of Time.

Early Muslims meticulously measured prayer times based on the sun — to honor this tradition, this clock signals your appointment through beautifully refracted light.

إِنَّ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ كَانَتْ عَلَى ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ كِتَـٰبًا مَّوْقُوتًا

"Indeed, prayer has been decreed upon the believers at specified times."

An-Nisa 4:103

The Clock

At the center sits the cube.

Each refraction a prayer window, shifting as the day turns. The sharp hands of time orbit it. No two moments cast the same spectrum.

وَطَهِّرْ بَيْتِىَ لِلطَّآئِفِينَ وَٱلْقَآئِمِينَ وَٱلرُّكَّعِ ٱلسُّجُودِ

"Purify My House for those who circle it, and those who stand in prayer, and those who bow and prostrate."

Al-Hajj 22:26

Toward Makkah

Of all the directions, turn towards what's best for you.

The compass will help find it. You'll know when you find it. Put on headphones — the call to prayer arrives from that same direction.

فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ ٱلْمَسْجِدِ ٱلْحَرَامِ

"So turn your face toward al-Masjid al-Haram."

Al-Baqarah 2:144
