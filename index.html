Introducing

a Gift of Time.

A study in light, time, orientation and a call to prayer.

A Gift of Time is a sadaqah jariyah dedicated to my daughters Nurjaan and Kauthar.

إِنَّ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ كَانَتْ عَلَى ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ كِتَـٰبًا مَّوْقُوتًا

"Indeed, prayer has been decreed upon the believers at specified times."

An-Nisa 4:103

The Clock

At the center sits the Ka'bah.

A first of its kind dichroic cube refracting the hands of time through an open door, inviting you to prayer.

وَطَهِّرْ بَيْتِىَ لِلطَّآئِفِينَ وَٱلْقَآئِمِينَ وَٱلرُّكَّعِ ٱلسُّجُودِ

"Purify My House for those who circle it, and those who stand in prayer, and those who bow and prostrate."

Al-Hajj 22:26

Toward Makkah

Of all the directions, turn towards what's best for you.

The compass lights up the way toward qibla. With headphones on you can hear the call to prayer from the direction of Makkah.

فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ ٱلْمَسْجِدِ ٱلْحَرَامِ

"So turn your face toward al-Masjid al-Haram."

Al-Baqarah 2:144
